# SCIENTIFIC FRAMEWORKS FOR INTELLIGENCE PROMPT V3
**Objective**: Develop a scientifically grounded prompt architecture for Forensic AI.

---

## 1. FORENSIC LINGUISTICS & STATEMENT ANALYSIS (SVA/SCAN)
### 1.1 Statement Validity Analysis (SVA)
**Principle**: Truthful accounts differ from fabricated ones in structure and detail.
**Application in Prompt**:
- **Undeutsch Hypothesis**: Truthful statements contain "unstructured production" (phi cấu trúc) but "logical consistency" (nhất quán logic).
- **Criteria to Extract**:
    - **Quantity of Detail**: Specific interactions, specialized knowledge.
    - **Contextual Embedding**: Events placed in specific time/space.
    - **Reproduction of Conversations**: Verbatim quotes vs. Summaries.

### 1.2 Scientific Content Analysis (SCAN)
**Principle**: Pronoun usage and verb tense reveal hidden intent.
**Application**:
- **Pronoun Shifts**: "I" (Ownership) vs. "We" (Shared Guilt) vs. Passive Voice (Distancing).
- **Tense Changes**: Past tense (Completed truth) vs. Present tense (Fabricating on the fly).

---

## 2. INTELLIGENCE ANALYSIS METHODOLOGIES (CIA/FBI)
### 2.1 Analysis of Competing Hypotheses (ACH)
**Method**: Do not just look for evidence that supports a theory; look for evidence that *refutes* it.
**Prompt Instruction**: "List 3 Hypotheses for the Subject's Intent. Systematically attempt to DISPROVE each one using the transcript data."

### 2.2 The Intelligence Cycle (FBI Model)
1.  **Planning**: Define the intelligence requirement (Context).
2.  **Collection**: Extract Entities (5W1H).
3.  **Processing**: Decrypt Slang, Correct Errors (ProtonX).
4.  **Analysis**: Connect patterns (Shadow Profiling).
5.  **Dissemination**: The EXECUTIVE BRIEFING (Administrative Style).

---

## 3. ADMINISTRATIVE WRITING STANDARDS (Vietnamese Context)
**Reference**: Decree No. 30/2020/ND-CP on Clerical Work.
**Style Rules**:
- **Subject-Verb Agreement**: Sentences must be complete. "Đối tượng A thực hiện hành vi..." instead of "Thực hiện hành vi...".
- **Tone**: Neutral, Objective, Authoritative. No adjectives like "đáng ngờ", "khả nghi" without evidence. Use "có dấu hiệu", "nhận định ban đầu".
- **Structure**: Inductive (Quy nạp) or Deductive (Diễn dịch). For Executive Briefs, use **Deductive**: State the Main Conclusion first, then supporting details.

## 4. PROMPT ARCHITECTURE V3 (The "Omniscient Investigator")
**Persona**: Chief Intelligence Officer + Forensic Psychologist.
**Input**:
- **Transcript**: Raw text.
- **Context**: Scenario (Fraud, Drug, Civil).
**Execution Chain**:
1.  **Linguistic Autopsy**: Dissect words using SCAN/SVA.
2.  **Entity Mapping**: 5W1H + Relationship Graphs.
3.  **Cognitive Synthesis**: ACH (Competing Hypotheses).
4.  **Administrative Encoding**: Translate findings into Decree 30/2020 standard text.

---

## 5. LESSONS FROM CONVERSATION HISTORY
- **Success**: Breaking complex tasks into sub-modules (5W1H, Emotions) improves accuracy.
- **Success**: "Shadow Profiling" (inferring hidden life patterns) provides high value.
- **Fail**: Bullet points in summaries make reports look "cheap" or "draft".
- **Fix**: Force "Paragraph Mode" for the Executive Briefing.
